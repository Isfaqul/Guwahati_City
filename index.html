Must visit places

Maa Kamakhya Devalaya

One of the most revered shrines of Goddess Shakti in India

Umananda Island

The smallest river island in the world, Umananda Island lies at the heart of the Brahmaputra River

Guwahati Zoo

The proud possessors of some rare and endangered species, including the pride of Assam, one horned rhino

Must eat places

Reboti Chat House

Established in 1987, it is of the oldest eateries in Guwahati. Reboti is an emotion for Guwahatians & you will never find this place uncrowded

Shaikh Brothers

A bakery that started off by selling bread loaves and biscuits since 1885, became a household name in more than just one city

Momo Ghar

The first Momo restaurant in Assam. Momo Ghar is said to be famous for selling authentic Momos in Guwahati
